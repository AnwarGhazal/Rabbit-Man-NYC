How you know where Rabbit Man's been?

Rabbit Man leaves his own stamp on his city, wheather it is graffitti,
or a beatiful mural on the side of a building, you know where he's been
when you see that rabbit logo. He always leaves a piece after he finishes
saving the city. The Police call him the Art Vigilante, and have quit trying
to catch him because he's just to fast.
