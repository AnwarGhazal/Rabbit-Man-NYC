Where can you spot the Rabbit Man?

Rabbit Man is a New Yorker, and can be spotted all over Manhattan. Rabbit Man
is known to hang out where you find good street food, like 116 st, and Park buying
a tamale from a mexican woman, or China town eating steam buns walking
down canal street. He is also known to hangout on NYC roof tops, where the cities
loud noises and sirens are softer on his super sonic hearing. His best friends are
Spider Man and Dare Devil, who are also roof top dwellers.
